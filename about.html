Dedicated to helping you look and feel your best through personalized care from certified and licensed medical professionals.

Our Story

Reviving Wellness in Our Community

At Revive Wellness NC, we believe everyone deserves access to treatments that can truly transform their health and well-being. Founded with a passion for helping others achieve their wellness goals, we've created a safe, welcoming space where you can relax and feel refreshed and revitalized.

We specialize in two areas where we've seen remarkable results: medical weight loss with GLP-1 medications and IV hydration therapy. Both services are overseen by certified and licensed medical professionals to ensure your safety while delivering effective outcomes.

Located in Knightdale, we proudly serve patients throughout the greater Raleigh area, including Wendell, Zebulon, Clayton, and surrounding communities.

Our Approach

What Sets Us Apart

Medical Expertise

All treatments are provided by certified Nurse Practitioners and experienced Registered Nurses. Your safety is our top priority.
