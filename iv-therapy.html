IV Hydration Therapy

Replenish, Restore, Revive

Fast-acting IV vitamin infusions delivered directly to your bloodstream for maximum absorption and immediate results.

What is IV Hydration Therapy?

IV (intravenous) hydration therapy delivers vitamins, minerals, and fluids directly into your bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system for 100% absorption. This means faster, more effective results than oral supplements.

Whether you're recovering from illness, preparing for a big event, dealing with chronic fatigue, or simply want to optimize your wellness, IV therapy provides the nutrients your body needs quickly and efficiently.

Benefits of IV Therapy

  • Immediate hydration and nutrient delivery
  • 100% bioavailability (vs. 20-50% from oral supplements)
  • Boosted energy and mental clarity
  • Enhanced immune function
  • Faster recovery from illness or exercise
  • Relief from migraines and headaches
  • Improved skin health and appearance

What to Expect

First-time clients: You'll have a quick RN assessment plus a 5-minute telehealth visit with a Nurse Practitioner to ensure IV therapy is safe for you. This medical clearance is $20 and valid for one year.

During your visit: You'll be seated comfortably in our treatment room while receiving your infusion. Sessions typically last 45-60 minutes depending on the cocktail. Feel free to bring a book, headphones, or your phone to relax.

Plan for: Allow 1.5 hours total for assessment and infusion for new patients unless otherwise specified.
