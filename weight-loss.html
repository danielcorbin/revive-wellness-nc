What is GLP-1 Weight Loss Therapy?

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) receptor agonists are a class of medications that have revolutionized medical weight loss. Originally developed to help manage type 2 diabetes, these medications have proven remarkably effective for weight management.

At Revive Wellness NC, we offer two compounded GLP-1 medications:

Important: We use compounded Semaglutide and Tirzepatide formulated by licensed specialty compounding pharmacies. These are not the brand-name medications (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound). Compounded medications are not FDA-approved but contain the same active ingredients at a more affordable price.

Compounded Semaglutide

Semaglutide is a once-weekly injection that works by:

  • Slowing gastric emptying to help you feel full longer
  • Sending signals to the brain that reduce appetite
  • Decreasing unhealthy cravings
  • Improving blood sugar regulation

Compounded Tirzepatide

Tirzepatide is a dual-action medication that targets both GLP-1 and GIP receptors, offering:

  • Enhanced appetite suppression
  • Improved insulin sensitivity
  • Greater weight loss potential in clinical studies
  • Weekly injection schedule

What to Expect

Most patients begin to notice reduced appetite and food cravings within the first few weeks. Weight loss typically begins in the first month and continues steadily as your dosage is optimized.

Common experiences include:

  • Reduced appetite and food cravings
  • Feeling full with smaller portions
  • Gradual, sustainable weight loss
  • Improved energy levels
  • Better blood sugar control

Is GLP-1 Therapy Right for You?

GLP-1 medications may be appropriate if you:

  • Have a BMI of 30 or higher (obesity)
  • Have a BMI of 27+ with weight-related health conditions
  • Have struggled with traditional diet and exercise alone
  • Are committed to making lifestyle changes alongside medication

During your consultation, we'll review your complete health history to ensure these medications are safe and appropriate for you.
